OUR STORY

Rooted in

Neapolitan

tradition

Osteria Vesuvio was born from a single conviction: that Italian cooking at its finest is an act of memory. Our recipes trace back three generations — slow Sunday ragù, hand-rolled pasta, and the briny perfume of the Amalfi coast.

Executive Chef Matteo Ferraro trained under the Michelin-starred kitchens of Rome and Positano before bringing his craft here. Every dish tells the story of a place, a season, a family.
